title: Savonet

h3. What is Savonet?

Savonet is a group of open-source developpers working on audio, with a focus on streaming and webradios. Savonet software is mainly written in "OCaml":http://ocaml.org. At the core of Savonet is Liquidsoap, the programming language that ties all the pieces together. Savonet also provides
"OCaml libraries":http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=89802
to the open-source community,
especially bindings of widespread C libraries such as
Vorbis, Mad, Lame, Jack, ALSA, LADSPA, etc.
